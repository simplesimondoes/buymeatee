# Quality Gate: Content

- [ ] `BuyMeATee` spelling is consistent everywhere (including metadata and alt text).
- [ ] British English throughout.
- [ ] Tone matches the brand: warm, aspirational, golf-literate, premium but approachable — never charity-like or corporate.
- [ ] Fictional examples are visibly labelled `Example`, `Preview` or `Concept`.
- [ ] Every claim is supported — nothing unverifiable.
- [ ] No invented statistics.
- [ ] No fake testimonials, reviews, users or partners.
- [ ] No premature feature promises (payments, apps, discovery presented as live).
- [ ] CTA language is clear and uses approved phrasing.
- [ ] The reader and their next action are obvious.
