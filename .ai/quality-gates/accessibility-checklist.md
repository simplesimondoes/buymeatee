# Quality Gate: Accessibility

- [ ] Everything operable by keyboard alone, in a sensible order.
- [ ] Focus is visible on all interactive elements.
- [ ] Semantic landmarks (header, nav, main, footer) are correct.
- [ ] All form inputs have correct, programmatically associated labels.
- [ ] Validation messages are useful and announced (associated with their fields).
- [ ] Contrast is sufficient (check cream/green/gold pairings specifically).
- [ ] Touch targets are practical on mobile (~44px+).
- [ ] `prefers-reduced-motion` is respected.
- [ ] Dialogs and menus (mobile nav, accordions) are accessible: focus management, escape, aria state.
- [ ] Images have appropriate alt treatment (descriptive, or empty for decorative).
- [ ] No information relies on colour alone.
