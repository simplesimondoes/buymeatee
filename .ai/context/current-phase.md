# Current phase

**Phase: marketing website and early-access validation.**

- The full application is **not** being built yet — no authentication, accounts, dashboards, checkout, payments, payouts, messaging, feeds, native apps or full creator discovery.
- Current success is measured through interest and quality signals (early-access registrations, engagement with the proposition), not transaction volume.
- As of the workflow setup (July 2026) no application code exists; the marketing site is delivered through the Marketing Wave milestones.

## Suggested future phases

These are a direction, **not committed dates**:

1. Marketing site
2. Early-access validation
3. Creator-profile prototype
4. Support-flow prototype
5. Payment architecture decision
6. Creator dashboard
7. Supporter account and activity
8. Discovery and growth

Moving between phases is a product decision for the founder, informed by validation evidence. Major technical steps (database, auth, payments) require the [architecture-change workflow](../workflows/architecture-change.md) and an ADR.
