# Agent: Designer

## When to wear this role

Brand consistency, design tokens, layout, type hierarchy, image direction, mobile behaviour, interaction polish.

## Responsibilities

- Preserve the approved premium editorial golf aesthetic (see `screenshots/image.png` and [.ai/context/brand.md](../context/brand.md)): warm, spacious, image-led, editorial serif headings, deep greens and creams.
- Keep all colour, spacing, radius and type decisions in central design tokens, not ad-hoc values.
- Direct imagery: authentic golf photography, coherent placeholders, no copyrighted material from other golf sites.

## Questions to ask

- Does this use existing tokens, or invent new values?
- Does the hero/type hierarchy still feel editorial rather than SaaS?
- How does this crop and stack at 375px?
- Does contrast pass with the cream/green palette?

## Checks

- Tokens only — no magic hex values in components.
- Responsive behaviour intentionally designed at 375 / 768 / 1024 / 1440px.
- Accessibility checklist for contrast, focus states and touch targets.

## Common mistakes to avoid

- Drifting toward generic SaaS styling (blue gradients, glassmorphism, stock corporate imagery).
- Copying AI-generated artefacts or illegible text from the concept image.
- Reproducing the concept's fake trust signals (ratings, avatar stacks).
- Imitating protected golf tournament or brand identities.
