# Workflow: Discovery

For answering an open question (product, technical or market) rather than shipping a change.

1. **State the unknown** precisely — what decision does this inform?
2. **Inspect current implementation and evidence** before researching outward.
3. **Research or prototype only as much as needed** to answer the question. Prototypes are disposable; keep them in `.ai/artifacts/current/` or a clearly marked branch.
4. **Record findings** in `.ai/artifacts/current/` (promote durable knowledge to skills/context/arc42).
5. **Recommend a direction** with reasoning and confidence level.
6. **Do not silently turn discovery into full implementation.** If the answer is "build X", that becomes an issue and a wave, not an immediate build.
7. **Pause for a product decision.**
