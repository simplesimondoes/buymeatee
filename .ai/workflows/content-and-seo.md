# Workflow: Content & SEO

For new pages, blog articles and significant copy or metadata changes.

1. **Define search intent and reader.** Who arrives here, looking for what, and what should they do next?
2. **Confirm the page does not duplicate another page's purpose.** Check existing routes and articles first.
3. **Draft useful content without keyword stuffing.** Follow the [content skill](../skills/content.md) — tone, terminology, British English, honest claims, labelled fiction.
4. **Add metadata and internal links.** Unique title/description/canonical; natural links to and from related pages.
5. **Add valid structured data where appropriate** — only matching visible content.
6. **Review claims and factual accuracy.** No invented statistics, fees, dates or endorsements.
7. **Check headings, snippets and mobile presentation.** One H1, logical hierarchy, readable at 375px.
8. **Run the [SEO](../quality-gates/seo-checklist.md) and [content](../quality-gates/content-checklist.md) gates.**
9. **Report and pause.**
