# 12. Glossary

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Creator** | A golfer with an audience and goals who has (or will have) a BuyMeATee page: content creators, amateur competitors, aspiring professionals, coaches, course reviewers, travel/women's/adaptive golf creators, juniors via a parent or guardian. Never "recipient". |
| **Supporter** | A golf fan who backs a creator or a specific goal. Never "donor". |
| **Goal** | A specific, visible objective a creator is working towards (e.g. Road to Scratch, Q School entry, a links trip) with a target amount and progress. |
| **Journey** | The creator's ongoing story — goals, updates, milestones — that supporters follow and participate in. The brand proposition is "Support the journey." |
| **Tee** | The smallest golf-native support unit ("buy a tee"). Scales as 1 Tee, 3 Tees, 9 Holes, 18 Holes. |
| **Support option** | A selectable contribution: 1 Tee, 3 Tees, 9 Holes, 18 Holes, Green Fee, or Custom Support. Illustrative until payments exist. |
| **Green fee** | A support option representing the cost of a round of golf. |
| **Early access** | The pre-launch interest list; creators and supporters register via the early-access form. |
| **Creator page** | The future public profile where a creator shares their story, goals and updates ("Start your page"). |
| **Supporter collection** | The future supporter-side record of creators supported, tees bought and badges earned (see app concepts). |
| **Wave** | A single-themed batch of delivery work, tracked as a GitHub milestone. |
| **ADR** | Architecture Decision Record in [09-adrs.md](09-adrs.md). |
