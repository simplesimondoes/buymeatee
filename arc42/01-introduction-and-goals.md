# 1. Introduction and Goals

## Purpose

BuyMeATee (`buymeatee.com`) is a golf-focused creator-support platform: golf fans support creators, aspiring professionals, amateur competitors, coaches and course reviewers as they pursue meaningful goals. Proposition: **Support the journey.** It must feel like participation in a golfer's journey, never a generic donation or tipping site.

## Audiences

- **Creators** — golfers with an audience and goals: content creators, amateur competitors, aspiring pros, coaches, reviewers, travel/women's/adaptive golf creators, juniors represented by a parent or guardian. Built for small and emerging creators, not only famous influencers.
- **Supporters** — golf fans who want to back these golfers towards specific goals (green fees, entries, travel, equipment, coaching, production) using golf-native units: a tee, three tees, nine holes, a round, a green fee, or a custom amount.

## Current phase

**Marketing website and product validation.** The system to build now is a public marketing site that explains the product, establishes the brand and SEO foundation, and captures creator and supporter early-access interest. Success = quality of interest signals, not transactions. See [.ai/context/current-phase.md](../.ai/context/current-phase.md).

## Long-term direction

If validated: creator pages, goal-based support flows, payments and payouts, supporter accounts, discovery. Each step is gated by evidence and an [architecture-change](../.ai/workflows/architecture-change.md) decision.

## Current non-goals

Authentication, creator/supporter accounts and dashboards, checkout, payments, payouts, messaging, social feeds, native apps, full creator discovery, databases, CMS.
